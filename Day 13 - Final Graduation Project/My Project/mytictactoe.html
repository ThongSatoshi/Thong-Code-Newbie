HOW TO PLAY

        1. Input your favorable board size
        2. Input player 1's and player 2's name respectively
        3. Click "Start Game" to start the game immediately
        
        FYI
        - Click on the board square to draw on the board
        - You can't go back so think carefully before you decide every move
        - Game continues until there is a winner
